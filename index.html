Field Glossary 📚
SFI: Solar Flux Index (Ionospheric "Charge"). Higher = Better for 10m/15m/20m.
K-Index: Geomagnetic Stability (Noise). 0-2 is quiet/best.
A-Index: 24-hr stability average. Lower = more consistent.
Prop Pulse Symbology:
SOLID/DX: High confidence skip path.
STRONG/LOCAL: Peak NVIS or Groundwave.
DX/TROPO: Peak VHF distance.
SKIP?: Likely skipping over your location.
WEAK: Marginal flux for this band/distance.
STORMY: High geomagnetic noise warning.
